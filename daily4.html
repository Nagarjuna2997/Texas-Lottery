📋 Detailed Game Rules

📅 Drawing Schedule

  • Four drawings every day
  • Morning: 10:12 AM CT
  • Day: 12:27 PM CT
  • Evening: 6:07 PM CT
  • Night: 10:12 PM CT

🎯 Play Types Explained

  • Straight: Numbers must match in exact order drawn
  • Box: Numbers can match in any order
  • Straight/Box: Win either way with one bet
  • Combo: Covers all straight combinations
  • Choose your bet amount: $0.50, $1, $2, $3, $4, $5

💰 Betting Options

  • Minimum bet: $0.50 per play
  • Maximum bet: $5 per play (Straight/Box: $1 min)
  • Combo costs vary: 4-way=$2, 6-way=$3, 12-way=$6, 24-way=$12
  • Fireball option adds $1 to any play
  • Sum It Up! option available for extra ways to win

📝 Important Information

  • Must be 18 or older to play
  • Tickets expire 180 days after drawing date
  • Prizes over $5,000 subject to taxes
  • Winners have 180 days to claim prizes
  • Play responsibly and within your means
