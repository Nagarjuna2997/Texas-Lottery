📋 Detailed Game Rules

📅 Drawing Schedule

  • Drawings are held twice per week
  • Tuesday and Friday at 10:00 PM Central Time
  • Sales close 15 minutes before each drawing
  • Tickets can be purchased until 9:45 PM CT on drawing days

🎯 How to Win

  • Match all 5 white balls + Mega Ball = Jackpot
  • Nine different ways to win prizes
  • Jackpot starts at $20 million minimum
  • Winners can choose cash lump sum or annuity
  • Jackpot grows until someone wins

⚡ Megaplier Feature

  • Multiplies non-jackpot prizes by 2x, 3x, 4x, or 5x
  • Costs an additional $1 per play
  • Match 5 white balls with Megaplier wins up to $5 million
  • Drawn separately before the main drawing
  • Does not affect the jackpot amount

  • Play responsibly and within your means
