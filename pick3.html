🎮 How to Play Pick 3

1

🎯 Choose Your Numbers

Pick 3 numbers from 0 to 9. You can choose the same number more than once (like 1-1-2 or 7-7-7) 🔢.

2

🎲 Select Your Play Type

Choose Straight (exact order), Box (any order), Straight/Box, or Combo for different ways to win 🎮.

3

Buy your ticket from any authorized Texas Lottery retailer 🏪. Tickets cost from $0.50 to $6 depending on play type.

4

📺 Watch the Drawings

Four daily drawings: Morning (10:12 AM), Day (12:27 PM), Evening (6:07 PM), Night (10:12 PM) 🕙.

🎉 Check Your Numbers

📋 Detailed Game Rules

📅 Drawing Schedule

  • Four drawings every day
  • Morning: 10:12 AM CT
  • Day: 12:27 PM CT
  • Evening: 6:07 PM CT
  • Night: 10:12 PM CT

🎯 Play Types Explained

  • Straight: Numbers must match in exact order drawn
  • Box: Numbers can match in any order
  • Straight/Box: Win either way with one bet
  • Combo: Covers all straight combinations
  • Choose your bet amount: $0.50, $1, $2, $3, $4, $5

💰 Betting Options

  • Minimum bet: $0.50 per play
  • Maximum bet: $5 per play
  • Straight/Box requires $1 minimum
  • Combo cost varies by number combination
  • Fireball option adds $1 to any play

📝 Important Information

  • Must be 18 or older to play
  • Tickets expire 180 days after drawing date
  • Prizes over $5,000 subject to taxes
  • Winners have 180 days to claim prizes
  • Play responsibly and within your means
