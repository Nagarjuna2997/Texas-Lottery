📋 Detailed Game Rules

📅 Drawing Schedule

  • Drawings are held twice per week
  • Monday and Thursday at 10:12 PM Central Time
  • Sales close 27 minutes before each drawing
  • Tickets can be purchased until 9:45 PM CT on drawing days

🎯 How to Win

  • Match 4 numbers + Bonus Ball for top prize of $300,000
  • Six different ways to win prizes
  • All prizes are fixed amounts (no jackpot)
  • Bonus Ball gives you extra chances to win
  • Winners receive prizes as lump sum

🎲 Number Selection

  • Choose 4 numbers from 1 to 35 for main draw
  • Choose 1 Bonus Ball from 1 to 35
  • Bonus Ball can be the same as main numbers
  • Quick Pick available for random selection
  • Up to 5 plays per playslip

  • Play responsibly and within your means
